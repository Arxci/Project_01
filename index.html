Who is Mountain View?

We've been the leading ski resort for over 20 years. We offer a wide array of amenities from lodging to food courts. For the last 10 years we've hosted the national ski competiton, seen by over 15 million people. Known globally for having some of the best ski locations ever, Mountain View is the best spot for skiing enthusiasts.
